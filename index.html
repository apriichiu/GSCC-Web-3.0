About

Good Shepherd Christian is a young multi-generational church in the Silicon Valley. We welcome all to our Sunday service at 11:00 AM, located at 940 S. Stelling Rd. Cupertino, CA 95104.
Beliefs

Good Shepherd Christian Church believes in the Apostle's Creed.
Pastor

Good Shepherd Christian Church is led by Pastor Joseph Chiu and his wife Jane Chiu.
Children's Sunday School

Our children's sunday school takes place during the Sunday Service at 11 AM. We have separate classes for toddlers, preschoolers, and K-6.
School

Good Shepherd Christian School is a Preschool that will start in August 2017.
